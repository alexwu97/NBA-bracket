How To Play

The NBA bracket prediction format:

Each match is a best-of-seven series, meaning the team that wins the match and moves on to the next round can only have a maximum of 4 wins. Each team's score is displayed here.

You can predict and enter the amount of wins each team will receive by selecting the score and entering the number. If you enter 4 wins, it will automatically move the team to the next round.

After you fill in all the predictions, submit your predictions by selecting the green button.
